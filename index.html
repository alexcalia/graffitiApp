Express Yourself

Having trouble getting out there and finding a place to express your creativity? GraffitiGram let's you choose a destination and show off your graffiti skills, apply filters to your work and download your creations to post on your socials.

Choose A Canvas

Find a location that inspires you to draw your best work. Choose a location from the drop-down menu and click the "Change/Clear" button to change the canvas. If you need a fresh start, just click the button again to clear the canvas.

Choose Your Tools

Switch between spray paint and a brush and change their size. Pick from a large amount of colours from the colour wheel.

'Gram It

When you're ready to show the world your creation, click the "'Gram It" button, choose a filter of youir liking and click the "Download" button so save it locally to your device.
